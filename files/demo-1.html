PROBLEMS THAT WE AIM TO SOLVE

Real estate investment, while attractive for its potential for wealth building and passive income, is not without its challenges. Here are some of the key problems that we aim to resolve at VISKO REAL ESTATE INVESTMENT PRIVATE LIMITED

High upfront costs

A significant down payment is typically required to secure a property, which can be a major hurdle for many investors, especially first-time ones. This also includes closing costs which can include fees for loan origination, title insurance, inspections, and other services, adding to the initial financial burden

Less Investment Opportunities

Some people are afraid of real estate investment debt because it involves taking on financial obligations that can be significant and long-term. General mistrust of financial markets, lack of market knowledge, fear of overpaying and investing large amounts in properties is what investors are worried about.

Market volatility

Real estate markets can fluctuate, and property values may not always appreciate as expected. Downturns can lead to losses, especially if huge investments are leveraged with loans

Management responsibilities

Owning rental properties comes with the responsibility of managing tenants, which can be time-consuming and involve dealing with repairs, maintenance issues, and potential tenant problems. Management responsibilities tend to increase when there is no technical integration

Legal and regulatory risks

Real estate transactions and ownership are subject to a complex web of laws and regulations. Investors need to be aware of potential legal issues such as zoning restrictions, building codes, and tenant rights

Time commitment

Real estate investing, especially managing rental properties, can be time-consuming. Investors need to be prepared to dedicate time and effort to managing their investments

Less Diversification

Investors investing huge amounts of money in a single property are not able to diversify their real estate portfolio

Liquidity Challenges

Real estate is generally considered less liquid than other investment assets. Selling a property can take time, and the process may be influenced by market conditions, location, and property type.

Market Timing Risks

Timing the real estate market can be challenging. Purchasing properties at the peak of a market cycle may result in lower returns or potential losses if property values decline.

2. Creation of Investment Opportunities

Through our mobile application, users can conduct thorough market research and analysis to identify emerging trends, potential growth areas, and lucrative investment opportunities. 3. Lower Entry Costs

Our application investing allows individuals to own a portion of a high-value asset with a smaller capital outlay. This reduces the barrier to entry, enabling investors to participate in real estate opportunities that might be financially challenging on an individual basis

4. Diversification

Our mobile application enables investors to diversify their portfolios by holding shares in multiple properties or projects. This diversification helps spread risk across different assets and locations, reducing the impact of poor performance in a single investment

5. Access to Premium Properties

Our application enlists and provides access to premium or luxury real estate that may otherwise be unaffordable for individual investors. This allows participants to enjoy the benefits of high-end properties without the need for a substantial upfront investment

6. Easy liquidity and exit options

Our mobile application also offers liquidity options, allowing investors to sell their shares or exit the investment before the property is sold. This can provide a level of flexibility and liquidity that is typically not present in traditional real estate investments

6. Income Distribution

Our mobile application allows rental income or profits generated from the property to be distributed among fractional owners in proportion to their ownership stake. This provides investors with a regular income stream based on the property's performance
